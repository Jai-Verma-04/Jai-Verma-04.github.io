Women Crime Dashboard

Created a dashboard analyzing crimes against women in India from 2001 to 2021.

  • Highlighted a 177% surge in domestic violence over two decades, with a sharp rise post-2005.
  • Revealed Uttar Pradesh accounted for 25% of dowry deaths by 2021, the highest in India.
  • Identified Maharashtra, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, and Tamil Nadu as contributors to over 56% of women trafficking cases.
